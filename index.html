Welcome, Mauritania Students!

This portal is designed for students from Mauritania who want to study in Russia. Find guidance on universities, applications, and student life!

Why Study in Russia?

  • High-quality education and global recognition
  • Affordable tuition fees and living costs
  • Rich cultural experience
  • Vibrant student community

Application Process

  1. Research Russian universities and select programs.
  2. Gather necessary documents: passport, diploma, transcripts.
  3. Apply online and submit documents.
  4. Await offer letter and apply for a student visa.

Student Life

Enjoy diverse campus activities, explore Russian cities, and connect with fellow international students.

  • Learn basic Russian phrases before arrival.
  • Join student organizations for support.
  • Budget wisely for living expenses.
